FAQ

Where do we ship?

Where you live doesn't matter to us at A.F.R. We want all of our customers to have easy access to our clothes and our brand, therefore we ship worldwide.

How long does the delivery take?

The delivery depends on where you live. We are based in Sweden, because of that deliveries within Sweden and Scandinavia usually take about 2-3 working days. If you live within the European Union it usually takes around 4-5 days. All addresses outside of Europa takes a minimum of 10 days and usually a maximum of 14 days depending on destination.

Where does the name A.F.R. come from?

Our name is part of the identity of our brand. Our motto is "Express Yourself" and we believe in doing so through clothing. Clothing is a sort of lifestyle and we see art, freedom and resistance as a part of that identity.
